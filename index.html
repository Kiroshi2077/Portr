WHAT IS PORTR?

A PORTR (Portable self-Operated Ripper of space-Time Rifle) is a handheld portal gun designed to get you from one point in space to the other in an instant.

WHY PORTR?

With PORTR you will never be stuck in traffic, be late for work, or have to get up for the remote ever again. With PORTR as long as you have line of site of a flat surface you will be able to instantly teleport there... ⮟

Portals created using your PORTR never disappear unless you shoot another portal of the same color or if you yourself close them, that means you can say goodbye to having to pay extra for luggage on a flight since now you can just put a portal in your lovely home and once you reach your destination all you have to do is put another portal down and voila, you now have a doorway right into your house from miles away.

You might be thinking "but cant anyone just walk into my portal and get into my house". Dont you worry! You can lock your portals at the press of a button. this works by disconnecting the link between portals making it impossible to travel through them. Closing a portal while you or someone is in transit between portals may result in injury or death. Aperture Labratories is not liable for any injurys or death that may happen while using PORTR

HOW TO USE PORTR

To use the PORTR simply insert your hand into the back end of the gun and pull the top trigger. This will release a blue beam of energy that will flatten into an oval shape when it contacts a flat surface... ⮟

Then aim your PORTR at another flat surface and press the bottom trigger, an orange beam of energy will shoot out which when it hits its target will link itself to the blue enery beam creating a rip in space-time which in turn a portal. Congratulations, you just created a portal using your new Aperture Science PORTR.
